You are modifying an existing production web app for Par for the Course. This is a digital tournament scoring platform with tournament director controls, player join flows, and tournament state management already in place.

Your task is to implement a sponsor card system tied to tournament settings.

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:

* DO NOT refactor or remove existing functionality.
* Preserve all current tournament logic, joins, scoring, player state, and TD portal functionality.
* Make surgical additions only.
* Mobile-first responsive UI is required.
* Maintain existing visual theme/style/components where possible.
* Avoid layout shifts and excessive eye travel.
* Sponsor flow must feel polished, modern, and intentional — not like a popup ad.

========================================
FEATURE OVERVIEW
================

Add a new “Sponsor Pages” feature that allows Tournament Directors (TDs) to:

1. Enable/disable sponsor interstitials for a tournament.
2. Add/edit/remove sponsors from the TD portal.
3. Force players to sequentially view sponsor cards when joining a tournament.

When enabled:

* Every player joining the tournament must view all active sponsor cards.
* Sponsor cards appear BEFORE the player reaches the scoring/tournament interface.
* Each sponsor card is unskippable for 5 seconds.
* Skip/Continue button remains disabled during countdown.
* After countdown expires, button becomes active and advances to next sponsor.
* After final sponsor, player proceeds normally into tournament.

========================================
TD PORTAL CHANGES
=================

Inside Director Settings / Tournament Settings:
Add a new section called:

“Sponsor Pages”

Include:

* Master toggle:
  [ ] Enable Sponsor Pages

When enabled:
Show sponsor management UI.

Each sponsor entry must support:

* Sponsor Name (required)
* Donation Type (dropdown or free text)
  Examples:

  * Added Money
  * Prize Sponsor
  * Food Sponsor
  * Venue Sponsor
  * Community Partner
* Short Blurb/About Company
  Limit: ~140-180 characters recommended
* Logo Upload

  * Accept image uploads
  * Resize/compress appropriately
  * Preserve aspect ratio
* Sponsor Order / Drag Reordering
* Active/Inactive toggle
* Delete sponsor button

Donation type should display visually as a pill/badge.
